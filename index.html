How it plays
Built around one honest tension:
order matters.
Trajectory preview

Press and hold any arrow to see exactly where it would go — a dashed line to the edge, or a stop mark at the first blocker. Releasing costs nothing. Only a tap commits.

Ghost slots

When an arrow exits, a faint outline stays behind. It keeps the board's shape in view so you can plan the next move without memorizing the whole grid.

Mistakes are recoverable

A blocked tap turns an arrow red and costs a life — it isn't removed. Clear the path that was blocking it and it fires normally. Nothing is a dead end you can't think your way out of.

A difficulty curve with teeth

Easy opens gently on a 3×3 board. By the Master tier you're reading 16×16 mega‑grids with nested circular dependencies. Level 100 is built to be worth a screenshot.

Easy Normal Hard Expert Master
A daily streak, kept quiet

One level a day keeps the streak alive. A small weekly dot tracker, a milestone bonus at 7 and 14 days — no push notifications nagging you back.

Hints, never the full answer

Stuck? A hint highlights only the single next arrow to fire — never the whole solution. Earn hints by playing daily, or unlock one with a short rewarded video.

No clutter, no catch
Quiet by design.

Arrow Escape never asks for an account and never interrupts a level with an ad. Rewarded videos are optional and only ever shown when you ask for one — to refill a life or earn a hint.
